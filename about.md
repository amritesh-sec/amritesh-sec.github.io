    <h2 style="margin-top:1rem;">Who I Am</h2>
    <p>I'm an independent cybersecurity researcher and advisor focused on the intersection of technology, privacy, and human behaviour. My work covers threat intelligence, data protection regulation, AI security research, blockchain forensics, and organisational risk.</p>
    <p>I publish research for professionals, organisations, and anyone who wants to understand the threats that actually matter — without hype, without speculation, always with primary sources.</p>
