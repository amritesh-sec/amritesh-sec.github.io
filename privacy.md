<h2>Our Approach</h2>
<p>This site is built with privacy as a first principle — consistent with the research published here. We collect the minimum data necessary to operate.</p>

<h2>Data We Collect</h2>

<h3>Contact & Submission Forms</h3>
<p>When you submit a form, your data (name, email, message) is processed by <a href="https://formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Formspree</a>, a GDPR-compliant form processor. Data is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not sold or shared with third parties.</p>

<h3>Analytics</h3>
<p>This site uses <a href="https://umami.is/privacy" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Umami Analytics</a>, a privacy-focused, cookieless analytics tool. No personally identifiable information is collected. No cross-site tracking. Fully GDPR compliant.</p>

<h3>GitHub Pages Hosting</h3>
<p>This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect server logs including IP addresses as part of their infrastructure. See <a href="https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement" target="_blank" rel="noopener">GitHub's Privacy Statement</a>.</p>

<h2>Cookies</h2>
<p>This site does not use tracking cookies. A single functional cookie stores your cookie-banner dismissal preference. No advertising or profiling cookies are used.</p>

<h2>Your Rights (GDPR / UK GDPR)</h2>
<p>If you are located in the EEA or UK, you have the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact via the <a href="/contact/">contact page</a>.</p>

<h2>Changes</h2>
<p>This policy may be updated periodically. The date at the top reflects the most recent update.</p>
